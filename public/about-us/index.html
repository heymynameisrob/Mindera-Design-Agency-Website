About us

Part of Mindera Group, we’re a dedicated product design agency built to be different. A small studio of designers, leaders and strategists that can help you drive breakout success.

A different kind of agency

Formed by a team of experienced designers and leaders, we started to challenge the tired old agency model of building products.

Our agency is different. We’re built to be lean and collaborative. We are dedicated to creating the best products and services, without wasting your time or budget.

Strike Design Labs are part of the Mindera Group, built to be a radically different approach to designing products.

Whenever a company hires an agency, they are plugging into a 40 year old mindset. Most of your budget is going on box-ticking, hoop-jumping busywork to keep the agency afloat. Really, they should function like a startup – an entrepreneur for hire.

Rob Hough – Innovation Lead
